Pause for a moment

Grepu is not here to turn you into someone who “meditates perfectly.” It’s for the days you feel rushed or drained—a steady breath as the doorway. No pressure, no judgment; just a gentle way to slow down and stay with yourself.

What the name carries

The Chinese name 归朴 echoes Daoist images of returning to what is plain, natural, and unadorned—setting aside extra polish and coming back to something simpler and more honest.

Grace

Gentleness

Permission to leave a little room for yourself before the next rush; no need to perform “perfect calm.”

Return

Coming back

You wandered off—then you return on the next breath. No scorekeeping for how meditation “should” look.

Pure

Clarity

Sometimes the present clears when we add one fewer layer of commentary on top of it.

What’s in the app

A minimal flow—from welcome to home, one guided breathing session, finish and share cards—so the barrier stays low and the time stays with the breath.

  • About two minutes and twelve breaths using a 4–6 rhythm—easy to fit into a busy day.
  • No account required; sessions and preferences stay local, quietly in the background.
  • Localized UI, with Widget and Live Activity support for gentle reminders at the system level.
  • Shareable achievement cards, haptics, and audio keep-alive—details that help you finish the session.
